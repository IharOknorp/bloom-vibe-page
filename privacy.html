Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

This policy explains how the BloomVibe website handles privacy, analytics, and consent choices for visitors.

What we collect

We only collect the minimum information needed to run the website and respect your privacy choices.

  • Your consent choice for analytics cookies and similar storage.
  • Your selected website language when you actively change it.
  • Aggregated visit and interaction data if you allow analytics.

How analytics works

If you accept analytics, this website uses Google Analytics to measure page views and on-site interactions.

Analytics is disabled by default and remains disabled unless you explicitly allow it through the consent banner or privacy settings.

Legal basis and consent

Optional analytics storage is used only on the basis of your consent. Essential storage is used to remember your privacy and language choices.

Data sharing and retention

If analytics is enabled, measurement data is processed through Google Analytics. Data retention and processing details are governed by Google Analytics settings and Google's terms.

Your choices

You can accept or reject analytics at any time and reopen privacy settings from the site footer.

You can also clear your browser storage to remove saved consent and language preferences from this website.

Contact

For questions about the website or privacy handling, use the contact details provided in the app publisher information on Google Play.
