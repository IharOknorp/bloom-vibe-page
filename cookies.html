Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

This page explains what storage technologies are used on the BloomVibe website and how you can control them.

Essential storage

Essential storage is used to keep the site working correctly and remember choices you make.

  • Consent preferences for analytics storage.
  • Language preference when you actively switch languages.

Analytics storage

If you accept analytics, Google Analytics may use cookies or similar storage to measure visits, page views, and site interactions.

Analytics is off by default and is activated only after your consent.

How to manage your choice

You can accept or reject analytics from the banner on first visit and reopen privacy settings later from the footer.

Third-party services

When analytics is enabled, data may be processed by Google Analytics as a third-party measurement service.

Storage duration

The duration of analytics-related storage is governed by Google Analytics settings, while your consent and language preferences remain until you clear them or change your choice.
